Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Story Studio ("the App") is a personal, locally-run tool. This policy explains what data it handles.

1. Data we process

When you connect a social account (e.g. TikTok) via OAuth, the App stores the resulting access and refresh tokens locally on the operator's own computer in order to upload videos on your behalf. The App does not run a remote server and does not transmit your data to any third party other than the platform you are publishing to.

2. What we do NOT collect

The App does not sell, share, or send your personal data to advertisers or analytics services. It does not collect data from any user other than the operator.

3. Platform APIs

The App uses official platform APIs (e.g. the TikTok Content Posting API) strictly to upload the operator's own videos to the operator's own account, within the scopes you authorize.

4. Token storage and revocation

Access tokens are stored locally and used only to call the authorized API. You can revoke the App's access at any time in your platform account settings, which immediately ends its ability to act on your behalf.

5. Data retention

Tokens and generated videos remain on the operator's machine and can be deleted at any time. There is no cloud database.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the operator at the email associated with the connected account.
